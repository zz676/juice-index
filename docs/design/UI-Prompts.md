# UI Design Prompts for Juice Index

Use these prompts with AI UI generation tools to create high-fidelity designs for the Juice Index platform.

## Global Aesthetic
> **Style**: Modern, High-Tech, Premium, Dark Mode default.
> **Color Palette**: Deep midnight blues/blacks (#0a0a0a, #111827) background, with vibrant accents in Electric Green (#10b981) and Cyber Blue (#3b82f6).
> **Elements**: Glassmorphism (translucent cards with ease-in-out blur), thin 1px borders with gradients, glowing effects on hover.
> **Typography**: Inter or Geist Sans. Clean, readable, professional.

---

## 1. Landing Page (Hero & Features)

**Prompt:**
> Design a high-conversion landing page for "Juice Index", a premium data intelligence platform for the Chinese EV market.
>
> **Hero Section**:
> - Centered layout.
> - Headline: "The Pulse of China's EV Market." (Large, bold, gradient text).
> - Subheadline: "Real-time tracking of Nio, XPeng, BYD, and Tesla. Production numbers, insurance registrations, and market share analysis."
> - Primary CTA: "Start Free Trial" (Glowing electric green button).
> - Secondary CTA: "Explore Data" (Translucent glass button).
> - Visual: A stylized, floating 3D dashboard mockup or abstract data visualization (lines/charts) glowing in the background.
>
> **Feature Grid**:
> - 3-column grid of glass cards.
> - Card 1: "Real-time Production Data" with a small line chart icon.
> - Card 2: "Weekly Insurance Registrations" with a list icon.
> - Card 3: "Supply Chain Analytics" with a network icon.
> - Each card should have a subtle hover effect (glow or lift).
>
> **Social Proof**:
> - "Trusted by analysts at:" section with monochrome logos of financial firms.

## 2. Main Dashboard (User View)

**Prompt:**
> Design a comprehensive analytics dashboard for a logged-in user.
>
> **Layout**: Sidebar navigation (left) + Main Content Area.
> **Sidebar**: Dark glass effect. Icons for: Overview, Tickers (NIO, TSLA, XPEV), News, Saved Reports, Settings.
>
> **Main Content**:
> - **Top Bar**: Search bar ("Search tickers or metrics..."), Breadcrumbs, User Profile dropdown.
> - **Summary Cards (Top Row)**: 4 small cards showing key indices (e.g., "NEV Penetration Rate: 42% (+2.1%)", "Total Weekly Insured: 105k"). Use green/red indicators for % change.
> - **Main Chart Area**: Large line chart showing "Weekly Deliveries Comparison: Nio vs Tesla China". Dark chart background, neon lines, interactive tooltip on hover.
> - **Recent Intelligence (Bottom)**: A split view. Left side: "Latest News" list with source icons. Right side: "Upcoming Catalysts" list (e.g., "Earnings Call - Nov 15").

---

## 3. Data Explorer (Metric Filtering)

**Prompt:**
> Design a complex data filtering and exploration interface.
>
> **UI Structure**:
> - **Header**: "Data Explorer".
> - **Filter Bar**: A horizontal row of dropdowns/multiselects: "Brand" (All, BYD, Tesla...), "Metric" (Sales, Production, Inventory), "Period" (Weekly, Monthly), "Region" (China, EU).
> - **Visual toggle**: Buttons to switch between "Chart View" and "Table View".
>
> **Table View**:
> - High-density data table.
> - Dark background with alternating row colors (very subtle stripes).
> - Sticky header row.
> - Columns: Date, Brand, Metric, Value, YoY Change, MoM Change.
> - Positive change values in green, negative in red.
>
> **Chart View**:
> - Stacked Bar Chart or Multi-line chart reflecting the filtered data.
> - Legend at the top.

---

## 4. News Feed & Article View

**Prompt:**
> Design a news aggregation feed optimized for readability and speed.
>
> **Feed Layout**:
> - Masonry or List layout of news cards.
> - **Card Design**:
>   - Source Label (e.g., "CnEVPost" or "Bloomberg") in a pill badge.
>   - Timestamp ("2 hours ago").
>   - Headline: Bold, white text.
>   - Summary: 2 lines of gray text, truncated.
>   - "Sentiment Score": A small gauge or dot indicating Positive/Neutral/Negative impact.
>
> **Article Modal / Slide-over**:
> - When a card is clicked, a glassmorphic slide-over panel appears from the right.
> - Content: Full article text, "Key Takeaways" bullet points (AI generated summary box at top), and "Related Tickers" tags.

---

## 5. Pricing & Subscription

**Prompt:**
> Design a modern pricing page with 3 tiers.
>
> **Tiers**:
> 1.  **Analyst (Free)**: Basic access. Gray border.
> 2.  **Pro ($29/mo)**: "Most Popular". Highlighted with a glowing blue border. Features: verified ticks, full history.
> 3.  **Institutional (Custom)**: For teams.
>
> **UI Details**:
> - Comparison table below the cards.
> - Toggle switch for "Monthly" vs "Yearly (Save 20%)".
> - FAQ section at the bottom (Accordion style).
